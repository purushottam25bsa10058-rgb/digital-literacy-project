 Cybercrime Prevention Checklist
 Basic Safety
Use strong and unique passwords
Do not share passwords with anyone
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
Device Safety
Keep your system and apps updated
Install trusted antivirus software
Use a firewall for extra protection
 Internet Safety
Do not click on unknown links
Avoid downloading from untrusted websites
Check website URLs before entering details
Email Safety
Do not open suspicious emails or attachments
Avoid sharing personal information through email
Verify sender before responding
 Data Protection
Take regular backups of important data
Use secure Wi-Fi networks
Log out from shared or public computers
 Awareness
Be careful of online scams and frauds
Do not share OTPs or bank details
Report suspicious activity immediately
 Conclusion

Following these simple steps can help you stay safe and prevent cybercrime in everyday digital use.
