 Cybercrime Case Study: WannaCry Attack (2017)
 What is it?

The WannaCry ransomware attack was a worldwide cyberattack where computers were locked and money was demanded to unlock them.

 What happened?
A harmful software (ransomware) spread across computers
It locked files and systems
Users were asked to pay money (Bitcoin) to regain access
 Who was affected?
Hospitals
Companies
Government offices
Individual users
 Impact
Many systems stopped working
Hospitals in the UK faced serious problems
Important files became inaccessible
 How to stay safe
Keep your system updated
Install antivirus software
Avoid clicking unknown links
Take regular data backups
Conclusion

This case shows that small security gaps can lead to big problems. Awareness and basic precautions can prevent cybercrime.
