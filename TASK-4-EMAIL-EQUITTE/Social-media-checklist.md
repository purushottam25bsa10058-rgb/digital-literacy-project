## Do’s:
Think before posting – Always check if your content is appropriate and respectful.
Protect your privacy – Keep personal information like address, phone number safe.
Be respectful – Treat others politely and avoid offensive language.
Verify information – Check facts before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.
Use strong passwords – Keep your accounts secure.
## Don’ts:
Don’t share personal details publicly – It can be risky.
Don’t cyberbully – Avoid hurting or harassing others online.
Don’t click unknown links – They may contain viruses or scams.
Don’t overshare – Posting too much can affect your privacy and image.
Don’t spread rumors – Sharing false information can harm others.
